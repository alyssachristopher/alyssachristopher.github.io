Traditional Sports

Special Olympics offer 23 different traditional sports that consist of: Basketball, Bocce, Bowling, Corn Toss, Cycling, Equestrian, Figure Skating, Flag Football, Floor Hockey, Golf, Gymnastics, Powerlifting, Roller Skating, Sailing, Skiing, Snow Boarding, Soccer, Softball, Speed Skating, Swimming, Tennis, Track and Field, and Volleyball.

Boston College has a Special Olympics team named the Double Dragons that compete in the Special Olympics Massachusetts every year. They play unified soccer in the fall and traditional vollyball in the spring.
